Why Work With Me

I bring more than a portfolio.

Empathy as a Practice

Being a mom changed how I design. Patience, seeing things from a completely different perspective, building for people whose context is nothing like yours — that's not a soft skill. It's the whole job.

I Invest in People

I've mentored 7-person design teams and 10+ students 1:1. I care about the people I work with growing. That's not separate from the work — it's part of what makes good work happen.

Curiosity Over Comfort

New domain? I'll ramp fast. New tool? I'm already testing it. I went from zero to full strategic handoff at LawnStarter in 90 days. Currently building with Claude Code.

I Show Up Off the Clock

Good coffee, long strolls, exploring new neighborhoods — I notice how spaces make people feel, how things are organized, what draws attention. That observational habit doesn't turn off at 5pm.
